About Me & The Course

Hello! I'm Chelsea Alexandre, a Computer Science major and Media Studies minor at Queens College. This portfolio showcases my creative journey in MEDST 241. Throughout the semester, I worked on projects in Adobe Premiere Pro, Photoshop, and Illustrator — learning how to communicate ideas visually, ethically, and artistically.

Video Editing Project 1

An introductory editing study focused on rhythm, pacing, and transitional flow using Adobe Premiere Pro.

Found Footage Remix Project 2

This project remixes found and archival footage to construct a new visual narrative through editing. By experimenting with pacing, sequencing, and juxtaposition, the piece explores how meaning can emerge from recontextualized imagery and demonstrates the creative potential of visual storytelling through montage.

Midterm Project 3

This narrative video explores the growing influence of immersive experiences in contemporary entertainment. Edited in Adobe Premiere Pro, the project combines structured storytelling and layered visuals to examine how interactive and experiential media are transforming audience engagement in the digital age.
